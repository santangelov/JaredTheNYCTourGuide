Prepare for the AP® Art History Exam with a Private Metropolitan Museum of Art Tour

Are you preparing for the AP® Art History exam and looking for an immersive way to study? JaredtheNYCTourGuide.com presents an exclusive Metropolitan Museum of Art tour designed specifically for AP Art History students. This will familiarize you with essential artworks, movements, styles, and history with an inductive approach, while the prepared student uses a deductive approach, describing the work.

Why Choose This AP Art History Met Tour?

  • Curated for AP® Art History Students – Our tour covers key works featured in the AP Art History curriculum that are in the collection, ensuring you see and analyze the most relevant pieces.
  • Interactive Learning – Engage in discussions, ask questions, and gain deeper insights into historical, cultural, and artistic contexts.
  • Memorable Experience – Visual learning reinforces exam prep in a way textbooks, illustrations and slide shows alone can’t match.

What You’ll See & Learn

During this Metropolitan Museum of Art AP Art History tour, students will explore iconic works from across time periods, including:

  • Ancient Art – Egyptian sculptures, Greek and Roman masterpieces
  • Medieval & Renaissance – Gothic cathedrals, early Christian icons, Michelangelo and Raphael’s works
  • Baroque to Modern – Caravaggio, Rembrandt, Van Gogh, Picasso, and more

Perfect for Students & Educators

  • AP Art History students seeking an engaging study tool
  • Teachers looking for an educational field trip option
  • Parents who want to support their child’s academic success

About Jared

I've been visiting the Metropolitan Museum of Art since 1978, worked there in 1985 for the Department of Twentieth Century Architecture and Design, studied Art History at Columbia University, and have been leading Met tours for nine years, including for students studying art.
