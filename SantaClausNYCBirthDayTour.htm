Santa Claus in NYC Walking Tours

Santa Claus the NYC Tour - the true story of an immigrant who made it here and changed the world!

We can visit where Santa and his reindeer were first seen, where his toy factories started, and where famous writers and illustrators brought Santa to life. Enjoy readings, poetry, and images.

You will be surprised about Santa's 3Ps: Patriotism, Peace, & Prosperity. This 'magical history' tour is factual.  It can be appropriate for Little Santa Believers with prior consultation.

The standard tour involves walking, stairs, and up to three subway rides. On private tours there will be opportunities to rest and refresh. We move at your pace and I can help arrange a Driver.
